About Us

Welcome to the Oswego 14U Twisters!

We are a dynamic and enthusiastic new team based in the greater Oswego, IL area, proudly fostering and developing young athletes through the Plainfield Athletic Club (PAC) system. Having just completed our inaugural 2024 Fall season, we are excited to continue our travel softball journey.

Our mission is to create an inclusive and supportive environment where teamwork and personal growth are at the forefront. We believe in the power of competitive play to not only enhance athletic skills but also to build a strong spirit. Our program is designed to help young athletes hone their talents, form lasting friendships, and develop a love for the game.

Leading our team are Coach Troy McCormick, with three years of PAC coaching experience, Coach Jackie Lopez, who brings 30 years of softball expertise, including travel and collegiate level experience and Coach Joel Lopez with his tenure as a softball coach and baseball player.

We can't wait to continue to support our girls' athletic journeys and build a softball team that champions sportsmanship and personal growth!
